About Me

I’m a designer, animator, and educator based in Accra, Ghana. I work across graphic design, motion, look development, compositing, and user experience, focused on making complex creative and technical workflows usable in real contexts.

I’ve taught visual communication and digital production at KNUST, managing lab systems and practical instruction for undergraduate students. Before that, I spent five years in multimedia production across creative execution and technical support. Teaching forced me to break down processes I’d learned through trial and error into steps that actually worked, and that shift still defines how I approach my work.

My practice centers on building systems, assets, and tools that hold up beyond the portfolio, especially in education, design infrastructure, and underrepresented visual contexts.
